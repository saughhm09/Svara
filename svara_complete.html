How to Play SVARA

🎤 Voice Control:

  • Make sound to lift the orb upward
  • Stay quiet to let the orb fall down
  • Control your voice volume to navigate through pipes

🎯 Objective:

  • Pass through as many pipes as possible
  • Avoid hitting pipes or boundaries
  • Score increases with each pipe passed

⚙️ Settings:

  • Sensitivity: How fast the orb rises with sound
  • Volume Threshold: Minimum volume to detect voice
  • Gravity: How fast the orb falls
  • Pipe Speed: How fast pipes move toward you
  • Child Mode: Custom adjustable speed for kids

💡 Tips:

  • Start with Easy mode to practice
  • Adjust volume threshold if too sensitive/insensitive
  • Use Child Mode for learning or relaxed play
  • Try different themes for visual variety
